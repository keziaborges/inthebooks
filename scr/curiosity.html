CURIOSITY

The first book I read was Pollyanna for a Portuguese class, I was 11 years old, already the first book I read other than for school lesson was Harry Potter and The Philosopher's Stone, I read 2 years after Pollyanna.

My uncles and my mom saw that I really enjoyed the first book of the Harry Potter saga and encouraged me to always keep reading, so they started giving me me books.

I've read more than 150 books of various types, and that popular saying "Never judge the book by his cover" is always taken into consideration!.

The Mortal Instruments saga was the fastest I've ever read, there were 6 books in 9 days, with an average of 364 to 532 pages.
