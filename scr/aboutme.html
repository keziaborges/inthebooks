ABOUT ME

My name is Kezia, I'm Brazilian and I'm 18 years old. I always liked those wonderful and different worlds like in the books. Each is an adventure and some bring us reflections on how we live our lives. I believe reading is really important for several reasons among them to create critical sense, culture, improve vocabulary and gain knowledge.
There is a bonus that you can get out of reality for a while and sometimes we need it.
I try to read in my free time and I like to learn new things, whether fictional or not.
